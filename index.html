Protection

Roof Waterproofing

Normally the main reasons for water leakage are cracks and pinholes in cement works. Our penetrating chemical floods into every micro-pore deeply to block penetration paths permanently after dryness.

Energy Saving

Heatproofing

Our heat-reflective coatings reflect all sunrays coming to the roof surface. Fans stop throwing warm air, resulting in up to 40% reduction in electricity consumption and keeping interiors ambient.

Non-Invasive

Bathroom Seepage

The main cause is urea and uric acid in sewerage lines. We clean and inject "Super Mega Leakage Control Chemical" by air pressure to fill all gaps from the inside out. No tile breaking required.

Potable Safe

Water Tank Lining

Concrete is less flexible and develops cracks over time. Conventional cement slurry fails within months. We apply professional crystalline technology specifically for water contact surfaces.
