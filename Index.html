Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers to help you make informed decisions about your property.

Epoxy is extremely hard and durable, making it ideal for heavy impact and chemical resistance in factories. Polyurethane (PU) is more flexible and UV-stable, making it better for areas with temperature fluctuations or exposure to sunlight, as well as car parks. We recommend the best system based on your specific site conditions.
We use low-viscosity epoxy injection. The crack is sealed on the surface, and injection ports are installed. We then inject liquid epoxy under pressure. This fills the full depth of the crack and bonds the concrete together, restoring the structural strength to its original state.
Rising damp is ground moisture absorbed by porous walls like a sponge, causing paint peeling and salt deposits. Yes, it can be fixed permanently. We drill holes along the mortar bed and inject a high-strength silicone cream that diffuses to create a permanent waterproof barrier (Chemical DPC). This is a 20+ year solution.
Yes! Because we use advanced chemical systems and follow strict application procedures, we provide formal warranties on our waterproofing and structural repair services. The warranty duration depends on the specific system installed, which is detailed in your initial quotation.
